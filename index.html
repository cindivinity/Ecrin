Something gets lost
Do you ever have a thought that brings you down during the day, and then later you can't fully remember it, but it still feels like it's there? Or a few thoughts come back all at once, and it's hard to make sense of them?
That's what Écrin helps you track.
How do you tend to make sense of things?
✕
Capture
Write what surfaces. No need to filter or explain. Even a word is enough.
Themes
Thoughts are grouped into themes. The app suggests a category and you decide what fits. You can rename themes and change their emoji to make them yours.
Look closer
Tap any thought to go deeper. The app reflects what the pattern may be about, how it tends to show up in behavior, and what it might open up. Consider the suggested reframes as a starting point. Then take what you find into journaling, therapy, EFT, tarot, or whatever practice you use to work through it. When something has genuinely shifted, come back and mark it as worked through.
Patterns
What repeats begins to reveal itself. Over time, the Patterns tab shows which themes keep surfacing and what tends to show up together.
A few more things
Long press any theme card to pin it or rename it. Use search to find a specific thought. When you mark something as worked through it moves quietly out of the active view. Nothing gets deleted.

About Écrin
There are thoughts that surface throughout the day, ones that linger, quietly, beneath everything else. These thoughts shape your inner world, the subtle layer that influences how you feel, choose, and move through life. Noticing them gently, without pressure, can begin to shift the way you see things. Écrin was created as a space to hold those thoughts, to bring awareness to what is often unseen, and to allow it to soften over time. Over time, what once felt automatic may begin to loosen, and things can feel a little clearer.
